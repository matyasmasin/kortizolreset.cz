Why cortisol

A tiny hormone with enormous power over your body

Cortisol is your “alarm” hormone. In short bursts it helps you. But when it stays high, your body flips into survival mode — storing reserves, blocking sleep and stalling weight loss.

Energy

Chronically high cortisol drains your adrenals. The result? Fatigue that coffee can no longer fix.

Weight & belly

Cortisol tells your body to “hold on”. Fat settles mainly around the belly and weight loss stalls.

Sleep

Instead of dropping in the evening, it stays up. Your mind races and you toss and turn past midnight.

Mood

Irritability, anxiety and a “short fuse” aren't a character flaw — often it's body chemistry.

Hormones

Cortisol overrides female hormones. Your cycle, thyroid and libido all pay the price.

Digestion & cravings

Unstable blood sugar triggers ravenous hunger and cravings that “willpower” can't beat.

The good news: cortisol responds beautifully to small, concrete daily steps. Those are exactly what you'll learn in the program.
